What Makes Our Codedegree's Interactive Platform Special?

The fastest and best way to learn programming is to learn by doing! So CodeDegre is a platform for learning to code by getting projects done in all programming languages. When you learn in action, you get:

Memorisation

First, we'll introduce you to a dedicated bookkeeper. They'll get to know your business, show you how Tenx works, and gather everything they need from you.

Mistakes

You learn from your mistakes immediately. Because you see with your own eyes what results from writing any code wrong

Encouragement

You can correct your mistakes quickly, and keep trying until you master it easily.
